IEEIA Project

The IEEIA Project was a group project. It called for us to address a current environmental issue. My partner and I chose the subject of dumping, which contributed to pollution via waterways. We were to create a survey to see how much others knew about the issue. In the end we found that a majority of the people did understand the harmful causes of dumping, We created series of webpages to showcase our results.
