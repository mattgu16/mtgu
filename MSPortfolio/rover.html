Rover

Perhaps the last project in middle school this is. The science project was to make a rover that would go to Mars and complete a certain mission. While most other groups in the class went for the RC car that would be covered by a box, we chose the "acoustic" route and made our whole rover out of cardboard and straws. I liked the originality of our rover, but it was not very functional.
